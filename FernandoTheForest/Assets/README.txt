
FernandoTheForest
Team5



Description:

Game for up to 4 players.



Controls:

	Player 1 uses keyboard and mouse.
		WASD to move.
		Mouse movement to look.
		Left Click to interact.

	Players 2...4 uses xbox controllers.
		Left Analog stick to move.
		Right Analog stick to look.
		A to interact.

	(Debugging) BackQuote (`) to switch control between the four players.
